Holiday oinsettia Cocktail

Descritpion

This is a nice holiday cocktail. We always serve this at our Christmas Eve open house. It has such a nice taste!

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces brut Champagne, chilled
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons cranberry juice

Steps

  1. Pour champagne into a champagne flute and top with cranberry juice.
