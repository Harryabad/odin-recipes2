Tequila Sunrise Cocktail

Description

A tequila sunrise cocktail has a sunrise-like appearance making it a welcome option any day! Garnish with an orange slice and a cherry if you like.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups ice, divided
  • 4 fluid ounces orange juice
  • 2 fluid ounces tequila
  • 3/4 fluid ounce grenadine syrup

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Fill a highball glass with 1 1/2 cups ice and set aside.
  3. Combine orange juice and tequila in a cocktail shaker; add remaining 1 cup ice. Cover and shake until the outside of the shaker has frosted.
  4. Strain into the prepared highball glass.
  5. Slowly pour in grenadine and let settle.
  6. Garnish and stir before drinking.
